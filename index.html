My Little Plug.DJ

Ponify plug.dj via Chrome Extension

Features

  • History over visited plug.dj rooms
  • Favorite plug.dj rooms
  • Ponifies the in the lobby and rooms
  • Ponifies some of the buttons
  • Add MLP background
  • Auto update to newer versions
